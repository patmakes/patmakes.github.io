Playing Live

Although you'll usually find me in a big room with a soundsystem playing my bass along with some throbbing beats, I also like to play a wide variety of different music with different artists in different settings. Currently I'm having a lot of fun backing up Toronto pop singer Kendal Thompson, you can find her album OK Cool on spotify. You can also sometimes catch me playing the blues with the ever-entertaining Brian Blain . I love collaborating with other artists, if you are in need of an instrumentalist, or are interested in Live PA set design click below to get in touch.
