Who I Am

I'm an instrumentalist/producer/music IT enthusiast from Toronto, ON. I've been playing music for almost 20 years (sheesh I'm old!), covering Classical, Jazz, Rock, Blues, and Electroic music in a variety of Countries, Institutions, and Establishments. These days I'm spending my time still enjoying my love of music, crafting my influences over the years into music with a unique sound for the new century.
